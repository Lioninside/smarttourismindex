Smart Tourism Index

A data-driven ranking of Swiss destinations by their value as a base for exploration.

SmartTourismIndex evaluates destinations as bases for exploration, not as endpoints. The central question is not how prominent a place is — but what it enables from there.

The index measures access to nature, lakes, cultural assets, and infrastructure, combined with the quality of the location and its current visitor pressure. This makes it possible to identify not only the strongest bases, but also those with real capacity to absorb demand.

What is the Index

A ranking built on access, not attention.

Overtourism is, at its core, a distribution challenge. Better data enables better decisions.

SmartTourismIndex evaluates 184 Swiss locations using a wide range of data, including geospatial, climatic, infrastructural, transport, and tourism pressure indicators. These inputs are translated into a single, comparable score.

Each location is assessed by its value as a base and what it enables — not by how prominent it is. The result is a ranking that reflects real access potential, base quality, and visitor pressure, offering a more informed and evidence-based alternative to popularity-driven decision making.
