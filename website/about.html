About this project

Smart Tourism Index is an independent side project by Clemens Bartlome. It sits at the intersection of tourism, data, strategy, and destination thinking.

Background

Why I built it

I have spent years working across Swiss tourism, international business, strategy, and transformation.

What interested me here was not building another travel site. It was testing whether public data could support a more useful discussion about destination value, tourism pressure, and access.

Switzerland was the natural first case. It is a strong tourism market, highly connected, and full of place contrasts.

Professional background

Project View

What I wanted to test

The main question behind the project was simple.

Can public data offer a more neutral way to look at destinations.

Not a full answer. Not a replacement for existing travel tools. But a useful additional view.

That is the idea behind Smart Tourism Index.
